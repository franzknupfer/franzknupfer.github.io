The Beginning

One night when Adan was meditating in the shining land beyond the pass, those high hidden valleys around Mount Pemo, the wind called his name. He was sitting cross-legged on a rock near the cave where his teacher slept, looking down at the icy lake in the valley below.

Come home, the wind said.

He hadn’t been home in twenty years.

He wondered if Linnea was happy in her marriage, and if she ever thought of him.

But he couldn't think about such things, not anymore. He was on the path to enlightenment and he'd given up on Linnea long ago.

About The Silver Thread

Adan is a monk who has taken the thread. After twenty years seeking enlightenment in the mountains around Diamond Pass, he returns home to the city of Jova to find that the woman he loved has become a widow.

Adan and Linnea have always loved each other, but he has his vows and she risks being burned to death if she pursues a life with him.

When an orphan boy appears on Adan’s roof, both Adan and Linnea are faced with a choice. Will he give up his vows for her? And will she risk her life to be with him? Will this strange child give them the family they always wanted together but never had?
